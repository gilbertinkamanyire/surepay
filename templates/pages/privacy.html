Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 18, 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you register, we collect basic profile details: your name, email address, username, phone number, and role. During your use of SurePay, we collect data about your course progression, assessments completed, and discussions participated in to calculate your statistics and ensure content is relevant.

2. How We Use and Share Information

Your information is primarily used to authenticate you and to provide, maintain, and improve the services offered via our lightweight platform. We share aggregated statistic data anonymously. We do not sell or trade any personal data.

Trainers can see the enrollment status, progress, and submissions of employees enrolled in their respective courses. Admins have access to system-wide data strictly for platform stability, moderation, and support.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption protocols. Passwords are computationally hashed upon creation and we do not have direct access to your plaintext password. You’re responsible for ensuring you use a strong and secure password.

4. Your Data Rights

You may request to receive an exported database file of the personal data we hold about you by emailing okiria.vincent@employee.utamu.ac.ug. You can also request that we erase any personal data we hold about you.
